Why Build Mobile Applications?

Why not just build a mobile website?

  • Have a brand presence in the app store
  • Enhance the mobile experience with native functionality (Camera,Geolocation,File System Access, etc...)

Two main reasons for building mobile applications are to get a presence in app store or to make use of native hardware.

Storing files for offline use.

Making use of the camera for taking pictures or scanning bar codes.

Geolocation - Getting access to the native geolocation apis for more accurate coordinates.
